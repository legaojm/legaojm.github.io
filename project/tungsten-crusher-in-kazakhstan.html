Tungsten Crusher in Kazakhstan

Kazakhstan is located in Eurasia and ranked as the ninth largest country in the world. It is also ranked the world largest landlocked country. What’s more, it is rich in coal, iron, copper and zinc and some other resources. Mines have numerous customers in this country.

Customer's Background:

In December, 2008, IDCO group Co., Ltd and the Ministry of Infrastructure of Kazakhstan signed a contract to mine tungsten in eastern Kazakhstan, which is the biggest tungsten mine in the Kazakhstan. The total contract is worth about 1200 million dollar.
The project is the largest contract projects received by Chinese company from Kazakhstan and is of great significance to the promotion of cooperation between China and Kazakhstan.
In order to finish this project on schedule, IDCO group Co., Ltd got in touch with us and purchased crushing equipments from us – Shanghai Shibang Mining and Construction Machinery.
