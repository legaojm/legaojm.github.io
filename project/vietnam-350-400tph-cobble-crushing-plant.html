Vietnam 350-400TPH Cobble Crushing Plant

Vietnam is an important mining export country in Asia, especially the exportation of Limestone, iron ore, coal, granite and bluestone products. With many years’ effort, Mines successfully developed Vietnam market, Mines crushing machineries and milling equipments are very popular in many provinces, like Yen Bai province, Ho Chi Minh City, Nghe An, Tỉnh Quảng Ninh and Hanoi. These mining equipments make up various mining crushing lines, sand making plants and mineral powder production bases.

Customer's Background:

During the 12th China Mac Fair-VN held at Saigon Exhibition and Convention Center in 7.2-28, 2012, the customer Nguyen Trung Binh came to our booth and got in touch with our salesman, Truong Van. After the first communication, he and Truong Van built good relationship, told us his requirements and required us offer one crushing plant design for reference. When our engineers were visiting his working site, they drafted one plan and showed to this customer and illustrated every detail clearly. Maybe this moved Nguyen Trung Binh, he finally decided to cooperate with Mines machinery company and ordered one 350-400TPH cobble crushing plant.
